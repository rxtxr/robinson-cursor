Pipeline (v4)

raw → flatfield → stabilize → deflicker (global) → deflicker (residual bilateral + motion gate) → nlmeans denoise + unsharp
  • Stabilize: ORB features + RANSAC similarity fit, per-pair delta clamped to ±8 px / ±0.6° / ±1 % scale to reject scene cuts and outlier animation; cumulative trajectory Gaussian-smoothed (σ=15 frames); inverse warp with BORDER_REPLICATE.
  • Global deflicker (v6 gentle): 64-point percentile curve per frame, temporal rolling mean over 21 frames, gain LUT clamped to ±25 %, 80 % blend with identity.
  • Residual deflicker: per-pixel temporal Gaussian (σ=5) + bilateral weight (T=12 gray) to pull flicker-sized deviations toward the smoothed baseline while preserving content. Motion gate samples neighbor frames at offsets ±1, ±σ, ±2σ to catch approaching and receding motion — prevents ghosts before or after character movement.
  • Denoise: nlmeans=s=4:p=3:r=9 + mild unsharp.

What works · what doesn't · what's next

  • gate-weave stabilization: ~σ=2 px of sub-pixel correction, no wobble-fighting on hand-drawn motion (the bug that killed vidstab in v1).
  • flicker — dark & mid greys: further reduced by the residual pass.
  • flicker — bright greys / mid-gray walls: now handled by per-pixel bilateral correction (was open in v1).
  • ghosting before/after motion: fixed by the 3-offset motion gate.
  • shadow / vignette: flat-field map recomputed over the full 7:40 sample.
  • cel misregistration: not yet addressed — background plate and character cel had minor mis-alignment on the original animation stand. Would require separate figure/plate masks, then independent registration.
  • upscale, RIFE 48 fps, audio hiss: open; would be a follow-up day.
  • dust / scratch: the source scan is already cleaned upstream — no extra pass needed.
