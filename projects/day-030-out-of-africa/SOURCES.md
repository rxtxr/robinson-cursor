## Source-quality bar (reminder)

- **Paper slot:** peer-reviewed primary source. DOI must resolve to a real publisher landing page. If unverified, leave as `"todo": true` in `events.json`.
- **Wikipedia slot:** English-language Wikipedia preferred for canonical landing; deep-link to a section anchor when relevant.
- **Video slot:** PBS Eons, Stefan Milo, NOVA-PBS Official, PBS, SciShow, TED-Ed, Royal Institution, Crash Course, ESHE-Society, John Hawks (paleoanthropologist, UW–Madison), university museum/department channels (Cotsen Institute / UCLA, OSU), Natural History Museum London, Smithsonian, BBC, UNESCO, FRANCE 24, History Hit, AncientCraftUK / Dr James Dilley, or credentialed-researcher interviews. Avoid Survive the Jive, AI-narrated content farms, listicle aggregators (Dan Davis History, NORTH 02, History Forge, History Time), and other ideologically-framed channels.
